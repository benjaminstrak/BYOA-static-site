## Properties of Llama Wool

- Warmer than sheep's wool
- Naturally water-resistant
- Hypoallergenic
- Available in natural colors

## Best Uses for Llama Fiber

1. Warm winter sweaters
2. Durable hiking socks
3. Cozy blankets
4. Felted accessories

## Caring for Llama Wool Items

Proper care ensures your llama wool creations last for years:
- Hand wash in cool water
- Use gentle soap
- Air dry flat
- Store in a cool, dry place
